How To Do ART.

Know when and when not to commit ART.

As all the greatest artists have always said, "If you aren't born with talent there is no point in even trying." Salvador Dali, DuChamp, Martin Lawrence... What doe all of these professional artists have in common. The fact that they were all chosen by God to make some of the greatest ART the world - and its people - have ever bared to witness.

The first step of being an Artist (provided you are not born with divine talent) is to just simply give up. This is the most effective and meaningful use of your time. Go to work. Be an accoutant and let the real professionals do the important genre defining, world changing work.

The truth that you are not willing to accept is that true ART can only come from the rich, good-looking, and intelligent upper-class of people who are down to earth and have to let the world know what they think. This is because those people deserve to be heard, you do not. What you have to say is just stupid and nobody wants to listen to that.So, do everybody a favor and just SHUT UP and get a job.

Art is only for smart people. If you are aren't smart just give up please, thank you.*
